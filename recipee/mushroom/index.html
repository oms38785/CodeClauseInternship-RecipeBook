Mushroom curry:-

Description


Cooking Time:-


Preperation Time: 45 Min       Cook Time: 20 Min       Total Time: 15 Min

Ingrediants:-

            
    
              
  •  button mushroom 250 gms
    • 
              
  •  Onions,finely chopped 3 nos
    • 
              
  •  Small Tomatoes,Purried 2 nos  
    •               
              
  •  Cashews soaked in Milk 10 nos
    • 
              
  •  Ginger Garlic Paste 1 tsp   
    •              
              
  •  Cream 1 tsp      
    •

Directions

            
    
              
  •  In a pan take oil and add onions . Let it turn translucent.
    • 
              
  •   Add ginger garlic paste and salt to taste.
    • 
              
  •   Add tomato puree and let oil seperate.
    • 
              
  •    Add salt, haldi, mirchi as per taste.
    • 
              
  •   Add mushrooms and let it cook without covering.
    • 
              
  •   Grind the soaked cashews to paste with little milk and add this paste to the curry
    • 
              
  •   When oil separates close the gas. A
    • 
              
  •   Serve with naan or chapati garnishing it with cream on top
    •
