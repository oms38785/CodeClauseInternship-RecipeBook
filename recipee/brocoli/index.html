Brocoli curry:-

Description


Cooking Time:-


Preperation Time: 45 Min       Cook Time: 20 Min       Total Time: 15 Min

Ingrediants:-

            
    
              
  •  10-15 nos broccoli
    •  
              
  •  1 tsp salt
    •  
              
  •  1/2 tsp red chilly powder
    •  
              
  •  1 cups malai
    •     
              
  •   1 tsp black pepper
    •

Directions

            
    
               
  • Take a bowl 
    • 
               
  •  Add broccoli and spices and mix well
    • 
               
  •  Add malai or cream and mix well
    • 
               
  •  Grill it for 5 min
    • 
               
  •  It's ready
    •
