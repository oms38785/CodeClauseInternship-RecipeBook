Brocoli curry:-

Description


Cooking Time:-


Preperation Time: 45 Min       Cook Time: 20 Min       Total Time: 15 Min

Ingrediants:-

            
    
                1 cups beans

                2 nos potato
                
                1 nos onion
                
                1 nos carrot
                
                1/2 cups coriander leaves
                
                1 tsp salt
                
                1/2 tsp red chilly powder
                
                1/2 tsp gram masala
                
                1/2 tsp turmeric powder
                
                1 cups oil

Directions

            
    
               
                Add oil in it
                Add potato, carrot and beans and fry them
                Take another pan
                Add all veggies, onion and tomato and mix well
                Add spices and mix well
                Add coriander leaves
                Mix well
                It's ready
